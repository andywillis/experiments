Okay! What do I get?

A well-curated package on a specific topic dropped off in your inbox every month. You'll pick up a new hobby, gain a new skill, do a fun project, or learn something just plain useful.

Amazing! What's in the "package"?

Several billion notes, book recommendations, background, how-to's, a million and one links for further study, and anything else we think you'd find half-useful.

Radical! Why are you doing this?

It's fun as heck learning how to do something new, but sometimes you just need a little nudge.

Marvelous! When do I get it?

The newsletter goes out on the 15th of each and every month. So, if you sign up right this moment: December 15th!

Fantastic! How much is it?

Two dollars a month, which is approximately 10.3% of the change hiding in your couch. Lasts longer than a cup of coffee, too.

Don't dig it? No big deal, you can cancel whenever you want.

Woo! Can I pick my hobbies?

No way! You won't know what it is until Dabbler hits your inbox. Secrets are more fun — think of it like a Christmas gift from a pretty cool uncle.

Wonderful! Can I see a sample?

Maybe someday, but right now we're riding high on the horse of mystery! His name is Professor Mystery Horse, PhD, and you should really trust him.

Sweet! What must I do?

You can go ahead and sign up through MailChimp! They'll send you a confirmation email and a link to Amazon Payments, then you'll be all set.
